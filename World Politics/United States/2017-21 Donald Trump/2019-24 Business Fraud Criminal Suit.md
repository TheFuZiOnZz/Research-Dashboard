- In 2020, the Manhattan District Attorney, Alvin Bragg opened a criminal case to determine whether The Trump Organization had committed financial fraud.
- In December 2022, two of the organization's subsidiary companies were found guilty of 17 charges including tax fraud.
- On 30 March 2023, Donald Trump was criminally indicted on 34 felony counts of falsifying business records in tandem with an alleged catch and kill operation to suppress negative press during his 2016 campaign, largely revolving around the hush-money payment to pornographic actress Stormy Daniels.

- On 30 May 2024, Trump was found Guilty of all 34 accounts of falsifying business records charged against him.
